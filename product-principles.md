# Product Principles

These 10 principles guide every decision, design, and delivery within Intelligent Agency's Product Practice. They define how we work, what we prioritize, and how we measure success.

---

## 1. Our process is our product.

We deliver repeatable, governed workflows that clients can own and operate. The value emerges from the process that orchestrates human and AI collaboration to produce measurable outcomes.

---

## 2. Outcomes guide everything we build.

We start with the business outcome, then work backward to design the workflow that delivers it. Features, tools, and agent capabilities enable the outcomes we pursue.

---

## 3. Economic impact defines success.

Success is measured in dollars saved or earned, not in adoption rates, user satisfaction scores, or technology deployed. If a workflow doesn't deliver verifiable financial impact, it hasn't succeeded.

---

## 4. Evidence enables scale.

We prove value in small, controlled environments before scaling. Evidence—quantitative and qualitative—builds confidence, secures investment, and informs iteration.

---

## 5. Capability transfer completes the work.

Our work isn't done when the workflow runs—it's done when the client can operate, troubleshoot, and improve it independently. Sustainable impact requires client ownership.

---

## 6. Human–agent collaboration is explicit.

We design workflows that define clear roles for humans and AI agents. Humans provide judgment, accountability, and context; agents provide speed, consistency, and scale. The handoffs between them are visible and governed.

---

## 7. Governance is designed in.

Controls, approvals, and compliance are embedded in the workflow from the beginning. Governance enables trust, reduces risk, and allows workflows to scale safely.

---

## 8. Measurement is embedded.

We build measurement into the workflow itself. KPIs, logging, and feedback loops are integral to the design and enable continuous improvement.

---

## 9. Systems thinking guides decisions.

Workflows exist within larger systems—organizational, technical, and operational. We design with awareness of dependencies, constraints, and second-order effects.

---

## 10. Client independence is a core outcome.

We measure success by client ability to sustain and evolve workflows independently. Our goal is to build client capability, creating lasting value beyond our engagement.

---

## Using These Principles

**For Executive Leadership:**  
These principles define what differentiates Intelligent Agency's product practice and ensure investments align to sustainable, scalable impact.

**For Product Working Group:**  
Use these principles to guide day-to-day decisions, resolve trade-offs, and maintain consistency across sales, delivery, and account management.

**For Product Practice Team:**  
These are your design criteria. Every workflow, feature, and deliverable should be evaluated against these 10 principles.
