## Roadmap Governance

### How Roadmap Priorities Are Set

1. **Client Success Analysis** — What patterns are working?
2. **Market Feedback** — What are clients asking for?
3. **Strategic Alignment** — What supports our North Star?
4. **Competitive Landscape** — What capabilities differentiate us?
5. **Capacity & Resources** — What can we realistically deliver?

---

### Roadmap Review Cadence

**Monthly:** Product Practice Lead reviews progress and adjusts timeline  
**Quarterly:** Executive Leadership reviews strategic alignment and priorities  
**Annually:** Comprehensive roadmap refresh and multi-year planning

---

### How to Influence the Roadmap

**Product Working Group:**  
- Submit opportunities through intake process
- Share client feedback and market insights
- Participate in quarterly roadmap reviews

**Executive Leadership:**  
- Provide strategic direction and priorities
- Approve major investment decisions
- Champion key initiatives

**Product Practice Team:**  
- Document patterns and opportunities from workflows
- Propose capability improvements based on delivery experience
- Share technical and market insights
