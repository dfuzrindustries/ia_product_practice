## Using Templates Effectively

### Best Practices

**Customize, Don't Just Fill In**
Templates are starting points. Adapt them to the specific workflow and client context.

**Maintain Consistency**
Use standard templates across all workflows to enable comparison and learning.

**Contribute Improvements**
When you improve a template, share it back with the team.

**Keep Documentation Updated**
Templates should evolve based on what works in practice.

---

### Template Request Process

**Need a New Template?**
1. Check if similar template exists
2. Submit request with use case and requirements
3. Product Strategist reviews and creates
4. Template tested and refined
5. Added to library and team notified
